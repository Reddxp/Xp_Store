The Hilarious Singing Debut of Casta

Casta, a lively young human with a love for music, always dreamed of performing. One night, while experimenting on his keyboard, he accidentally pressed a combination of keys that produced a crazy catchy beat. Without thinking, he started singing along—and somehow his notes sounded amazing!

His friends, hearing the fun performance, ran over to watch. Casta, energized by their laughter, spun around dramatically, hitting high notes like a neon superstar in the night city.

By the end, everyone was clapping, calling him the “Night Beat Legend.” Casta realized that sometimes accidents make the best performances—and the neon-red night just made it cooler!
